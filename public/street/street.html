Hello!

We are Tove and Ellen, sitting closest to the camera in this picture. We are musicians and have just started playing on the streets. We like to play a variation of genres and instruments, now we play lots of our favorite jazz, 70s folk and bossa songs.

Since you have found this page we assume that you have seen us on playing on the street :-D If you want to book us to play we would love to hear from you, please email us at toveama@gmail.com

And if you want to tip us here is a link to our Revolut account: revolut.me/toveandersson thank you!

You could also support us by buying posters or earrings from my (Tove's) small business, on this website. It's all handmade with recycled materials and environment-friendly printing.

We have a limited storage with posters and earrings with us on our busking trips, so you can also come up to us between our sets or songs. Our sets are usually around 25 min long, pay in cash or Revolut.

NOTE that this the website has no link to this page, since it is specific for our street performances, so if you would like to save information on this please remember the url tove.dev/street and search for it in a webbrowser, or save this page. We appriciate your intrest!
