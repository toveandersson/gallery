Hello!

We are Ellen and Tove, two musicians from Sweden. We like to play a variation of genres and instruments, and have just recently started busking:) Right now our repertoir mainly consists of our favorite jazz, 70s folk and bossa songs.

Since you have found this page we assume that you have seen us playing on the street :-D If you have questions or want to book us we would love to hear from you! Mail us at toveama@gmail.com

If you want to tip us here are our links:

Thank you! :)

You could also support us by buying posters or earrings from Tove's small business, on this website or in person. It's all handmade with recycled materials and environment-friendly printing.

We have a limited storage with posters and earrings with us on these busking trips, feel free to come up to us and ask in between our sets or songs!:) Or mail us at toveama@gmail.com

This website has all the available posters on it, but it is not yet possible to buy them through the website.

NOTE that this the website has no link to this page, since it is specific for our street performances, so if you would like to save information on this please remember the URL tove.dev/street and search for it in a web browser, or save this page. We appreciate your interest!
